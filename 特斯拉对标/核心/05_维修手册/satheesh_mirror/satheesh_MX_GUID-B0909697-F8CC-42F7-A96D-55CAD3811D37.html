Tweeter - Rear Door - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 21212702

Removal

  1. Remove the rear speaker grille (refer to procedure).
  2. Remove the rear door trim panel (refer to procedure).
  3. Disconnect the electrical connector from the bottom of the tweeter.
    Note: Components have been removed in this graphic to aid clarity.

    View large image.

  4. Gently release the tweeter from the retaining clips and maneuver it out from the door panel.
    Note: Components have been removed in this graphic to aid clarity.

    View large image.

  5. Remove the tweeter from the vehicle.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal.
