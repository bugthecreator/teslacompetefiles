Bracket - Rear Door Trim - Center - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 15135202

Removal

  1. Open the LH rear door.
  2. Remove the LH rear door upper trim panel (refer to procedure).
  3. Remove the screws (x4) that secure the middle upper bracket (torque 3 Nm).
  4. Remove the bolts (x3) that secure the middle upper bracket (torque 4 Nm).
  5. Remove the bracket.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal.
