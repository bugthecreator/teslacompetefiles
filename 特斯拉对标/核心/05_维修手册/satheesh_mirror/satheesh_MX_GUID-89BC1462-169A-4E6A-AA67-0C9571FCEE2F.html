Module - Ultrasonic Door Sensors - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 11334502

Removal

  1. Remove the rear door trim panel (refer to procedure).
  2. Remove the screws (x2) that secure the module to the door (torque 1.5 Nm).

    View large image.

  3. Disconnect the electrical connectors (x2) from the bottom of the module.
    Note: Components have been removed in this graphic to aid clarity.

    View large image.

  4. Remove the module from the vehicle.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal.
