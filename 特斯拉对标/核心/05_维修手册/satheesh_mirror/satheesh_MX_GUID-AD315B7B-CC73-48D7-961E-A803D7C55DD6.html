Seal - Door - Rear - Upper - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 11706002

Removal

  1. Remove the upper rear door glass (refer to procedure).
  2. Remove the seal from the upper rear door glass.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal, except for the following:

  1. Remove any remaining residue and clean the affected area with alcohol.
  2. When installing the seal, ensure that the notches in the seal align with the hinges on the door.
