Bezel - Exterior Door Handle - Rear - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 11451502

Removal

  1. Open the front door.
  2. Use a plastic trim tool to slide the rear exterior door handle bezel outboard and towards the front of the vehicle.
  3. Slide the exterior door handle bezel towards the front of the vehicle until it is removed.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal.
