Motor - Seat Track - 2nd Row - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 13021002

Removal

  1. Remove the LH outer seat track (refer to procedure).
  2. Remove the fasteners that secure the motor to the side of the seat (torque 29 Nm).

    View large image.

  3. Disconnect the seat motor harness.

    View large image.

  4. Remove the fasteners that secure the motor to the seat (torque 23 Nm).

    View large image.

  5. Remove the seat track motor from the seat.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal.
