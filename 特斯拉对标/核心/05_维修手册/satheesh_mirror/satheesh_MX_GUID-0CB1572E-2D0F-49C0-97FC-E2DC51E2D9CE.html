Seal - Door Front Outer Belt - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 11703002

Remove

  1. Lower the front and rear LH windows.
  2. Open the front door.
  3. Partially open the rear door.
  4. Use a plastic trim tool to release the outer belt seal.

    View large image.

  5. Remove the outer belt seal from the vehicle.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal.
