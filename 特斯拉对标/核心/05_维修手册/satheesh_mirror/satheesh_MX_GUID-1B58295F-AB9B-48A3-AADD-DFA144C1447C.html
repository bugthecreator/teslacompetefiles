Door Glass - LH - Front (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 11500102

Remove

  1. Remove the LH front door closeout panel (refer to procedure).
  2. Remove the inner belt seal (refer to procedure).
  3. Remove the outer belt seal (refer to procedure).
  4. Reconnect the front door trim panel harness to the front door module, then fully raise the window. Do not disconnect the harness at this time.
  5. Release the nut that secures the window to the regulator (torque 9 Nm).
    Note: It is not necessary to fully remove the nut.

    View large image.

  6. Lower the window for access to the regulaor tab.
  7. Push in on the tab on the regulator, then lift the glass out of the door.
    Caution: Take care not to damage component(s).

    View large image.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal, except for the following:

Caution: When installing the window, do not insert the glass into the guide channel. Instead, insert the metal guide plate on the window into the channel.
