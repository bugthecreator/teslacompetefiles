Strut - Rear Door - Secondary - Forward - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 11331602

Removal

  1. Apply protective tape onto the racetrack trim to prevent damage.
  2. Remove the front upper rear door trim bracket (refer to procedure).
  3. Support the rear door.

Warning: Ensure that the door is fully supported before continuing. Either have an assistant support the full weight of the door or use a pole jack.

  1. Release the strut from the body, the rotate the strut outwards.
  2. Release the strut from the door.
  3. Disconnect the secondary strut harness.
  4. Release the clips that secure the harness.
  5. Remove the strut from the vehicle.
Warning: Continue to support the door until the new strut is installed.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal, except for the following:

Working from the front row, press and hold the B-Pillar rear door switch until the door fully cinches closed.
