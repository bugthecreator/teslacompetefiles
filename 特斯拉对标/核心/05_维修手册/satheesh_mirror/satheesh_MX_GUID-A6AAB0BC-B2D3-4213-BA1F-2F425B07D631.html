Latch and Cinch Motor - Door - Rear - LH (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 11351502

Removal

  1. Remove the closeout panel (refer to procedure).
  2. Remove the bolts (x3) that secure the cinch motor to the door (torque 3 Nm).

    View large image.

  3. Remove the rear speaker (refer to procedure).
  4. Disconnect the electrical connector from the door latch.

    View large image.

  5. Remove the screws (x2) that secure the door latch cover (torque 22 Nm). Remove the door latch cover.

    View large image.

  6. Lift the cinch motor and disconnect the electrical connector.

    View large image.

  7. Remove the rear door latch and cinch motor assembly from the vehicle.

    View large image.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal.
