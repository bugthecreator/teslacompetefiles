Garnish - Door Trim - LH - Rear - Lower (Remove and Replace)

FRT No: 15132502

Removal

  1. Open the rear door.
  2. Lower the window.
  3. Use a trim tool to carefully release the clips (x10) that secure the lower door trim garnish.
    Caution: Take care not to damage component(s).

    View large image.

  4. Remove the lower door trim garnish from the vehicle.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal, except for the following:
Caution: Replace any broken clips.
Tip: Use the dowel pin in the center of the door garnish to align it to the door.
Note: Ensure that the garnish is fully secured.
