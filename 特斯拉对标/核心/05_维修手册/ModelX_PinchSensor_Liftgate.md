1135 - Latches, Strikers, and Pinch Sensors

Removal

Open the liftgate.

Remove the pushpin clips (x3) that
            attach the LH and RH side pinch sensors to the liftgate.

Disconnect the forward edge pinch
            sensor from the LH side pinch sensor assembly.

Remove the forward edge liftgate pinch
            sensor from the rear lift gate glass.

Informational Purposes

An informational icon, calling your attention

Note

The forward edge pinch sensor is
              attached to the glass with double sided tape.

Installation

Installation procedure is the reverse of removal, except for the following:

Wipe off all adhesive residue from the
            rear lift gate glass.

Add some adhesive promoter to the rear
            upper lift gate glass area, and allow it to dry to the touch.

Remove the tape backing from the pinch
            sensor and start attaching the sensor to the rear lift gate. Start on the right side
            moving to the left.

Close the rear lift gate and depress
            the sensor to confirm it is working correctly.

Warning Icon

A warning icon, calling your attention to a possibly risky situation

CAUTION

Stop the lift gate
              with the other hand in case the new sensor is not working.
