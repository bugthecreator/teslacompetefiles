    Moving Glass - Door - Front - LH (Remove and Install)

    Correction code 1020030101 FRT 0.18 NOTE: Unless otherwise explicitly stated in the procedure, the correction code and FRT listed above reflect all of the work required to perform this procedure, including the linked procedures. Do not stack correction codes unless explicitly told to do so. NOTE: See Flat Rate Times to learn more about FRTs and how they are created. NOTE: See Personal Protection to make sure you are wearing proper PPE when performing the procedure below. NOTE: See Ergonomic Precautions for safe and healthy working practices.

    • 2024-12-11: Added new caution about Pre-Installation Glass Inspection service requirements.
    • 2024-10-17: Update glass to regulator torque to 4 Nm.
    • 2024-05-13: Added step and warning to support glass while removing regulator fasteners.
    Warning
    This procedure is a DRAFT. Although it has been validated, Warnings and Cautions might be missing. Follow safety requirements and use extreme caution when working on or near High Voltage systems and components.
    CAUTION
    New glass components (windshield, roof, door, and fixed glass) must be inspected before installation to meet Teslaâs internal standards of quality. Perform the inspection described in Pre-Installation Glass Inspection before beginning this procedure.
    Parent topic: 1020 - Windshield and Body Glass

    Remove

    1. Remove the LH front door trim panel. See Panel - Door Trim - Front - LH (Remove and Install).
    2. Connect an external window switch.
    3. Use a pocket screwdriver to manually latch the LH front door.
    4. Raise the LH front window.
    5. Release the rubber datums (x6) to remove the LH front door inner waist belt seal from the door assembly.
    6. Remove the LH front door grommets (x4).
    7. With the help of an assistant, hold the door glass in position before continuing to the next step.
      Warning
      The clamps do not hold the entire regulator base. Failure to support the glass while removing the fasteners in the next step may result in the glass slipping off the regulator.
    8. With the assistant supporting the glass, remove the bolts (x2) that attach the LH front door moving glass to the regulator, and then remove the glass from the door.
      Warning
      Make sure to support the glass while removing the fasteners. The clamps do not hold the entire base of the regulator.
      TIp
      Use of the following tool(s) is recommended:
      • 10 mm socket

    Install

    1. Position the LH front door moving glass into the LH front door, and then install and hand tighten the bolts (x2) that attach the glass from the regulator.
      Note
      Be careful of the glass slipping off of the mount.
      TIp
      Use of the following tool(s) is recommended:
      • 10 mm socket
    2. Manually release the LH front door latch.
      Note
      Pull the manual release cable away from the vehicle to unlatch.
    3. Close the LH front door.
    4. Check and adjust the gap and flushness of the LH front door moving glass.
      Note
      Connect a external window switch to help with adjustment if needed.
    5. Open the LH front door.
    6. Torque the bolts (x2) that attach the LH front door moving glass to the regulator, and then install the grommets (x2).
      12 Nm (8.8 lbs-ft)
      TIp
      Use of the following tool(s) is recommended:
      • 10 mm socket
    7. Install the LH front door inner waist belt seal.
    8. Install the LH front door trim panel. See Panel - Door Trim - Front - LH (Remove and Install).
    9. Calibrate the LH front window.
      Note
      Press and hold down the selected window for 15 seconds. Raise the window and hold for 15 seconds. Test window by hard pressing to verify auto raise/lower functions correctly.
    10. Raise the LH front window and close the LH front door.
