Comfortable, safe and efficient – Excellence Inside.

Adjustment systems for seats

Electric motors and drives

Future mobility experiences

Autonomous driving, electrification and connectivity are completely reshaping the way we get around. The desire for more individuality, flexibility and comfort is becoming increasingly important. To meet these expectations, we combine our mechatronic components with sensor technology and powerful software to smart systems. In this way, we create innovative functions and enable completely new mobility experiences.

Intelligent access

When approaching, the car recognizes its driver and greet them via their smartphone. It communicates wirelessly with the vehicle, which automatically opens the driver’s door. At the same time, the steering wheel and seat adjust for comfortable entry. This access experience is made possible by combining our expertise in vehicle access and interior.

Portfolio Exterior

Adaptable interior

In future, vehicle users will have even more control over how they want to organize their journey. Whether they want to work, relax or drive themselves - electronically controlled seats, screens and storage compartments adapt individually and ensure an optimally adjusted position. Furthermore, Brose's seat electronics offer additional comfort functions such as seat heating and massage to make the driving experience even more pleasant.

Portfolio Interior

Efficient drives

In electric vehicles, setting the individual comfort temperature can significantly reduce the range, as the air conditioning of the interior and the battery consume the most energy alongside the drive. However, our electric auxiliary units make a significant contribution to increasing the efficiency of hybrid and electric vehicles. The reduced energy consumption noticeably increases the range.
