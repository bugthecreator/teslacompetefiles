Press releases

Micromobility: greater efficiency through new semiconductor technology

As part of the MicroGaN research project, we are collaborating with Coburg University and Sumida Components & Modules GmbH to significantly improve powertrains for light vehicles. In doing so, we are driving technological progress in the field of micromobility and thereby strengthening its role in this rapidly growing global market of the future.

Brose stories

Green and in demand: Micromobility is growing worldwide

To advance the development of efficient drive systems for micromobility, Brose has launched the research project MicroGaN. The project examines the use of gallium nitride power semiconductors in combination with conventional silicon-based power electronics.
